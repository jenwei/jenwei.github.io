Olin Conductorless Orchestra

At Olin, I am part of OCO, the Conductorless Orchestra on campus. Because we are conductorlesss, we lead together - choosing our own pieces, conducting ourselves, and running our own practices. This is my second year in OCO, where I play the violin. I am also a transcriber for OCO, so after we select pieces, I take the original scores and essentially rewrite them in Sibelius so that we can then reorchestrate it for our unique group.
